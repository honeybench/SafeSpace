Mental Health Tips

🌿 Mental & Emotional Tips

  • Take deep breaths when you feel overwhelmed (like the 4-7-8 Breathing Technique)
  • Write your thoughts in a journal
  • Remind yourself that bad days don’t last forever
  • It’s okay to not be okay sometimes
  • Don’t be too hard on yourself
  • 🧘‍♂️ Practice the 3-3-3 Rule for Anxiety (Grounding Technique)
    • 3 Things You See: Look around and identify three distinct objects in your immediate environment.
    • 3 Things You Hear: Listen closely and identify three sounds you can pick up in your surroundings (e.g., a fan, distant traffic, or birds).
    • 3 Things You Touch: Focus on three physical textures or sensations you feel (e.g., your shirt fabric, the chair beneath you, or the ground under your feet).

🧠 Study & Academic Stress Tips

  • Break tasks into smaller steps
  • Avoid cramming—review little by little
  • Make a simple daily schedule
  • Reward yourself after finishing tasks

😌 Relaxation & Self-Care Tips

  • Listen to music you enjoy 🎧
  • Take a short walk outside 🌲
  • Drink water and eat properly
  • Try stretching or light exercise (like Progressive Muscle Relaxation)
  • Take a break from social media (Digital Detox)
